About Us

  • Home

Hydevest is a leading supplier of premium cowhides, dedicated to serving retailers, processors, and manufacturers across diverse industries. We specialize in delivering high-quality air-dried cowhides, ideal for food-related processing and general consumption, as well as wet-salted hides, expertly preserved for premium leather production. Our commitment is to provide dependable, consistent materials that meet the exact standards of our clients, regardless of scale or sector.
